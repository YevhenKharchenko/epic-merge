FAQ

  • How do I play Epic Merge?

    Merge identical units to create stronger ones, deploy your army, and let them battle automatically.

  • What happens if I don't merge my units?

    Weaker units remain on the field. Merging improves their power, helping you defeat stronger enemies.

  • Is there a best strategy for winning battles?

    The key is balancing archers, knights, and other troops. Experiment with different combinations for the best results.

  • Does the game require an internet connection?

    No, Epic Merge can be played offline, but certain features like leaderboards require connectivity.

  • Are there in-game purchases?

    Yes, you can purchase boosts and upgrades, but all units can be unlocked through gameplay.

  • Do battles get harder over time?

    Yes, as you progress, enemies become stronger, requiring more advanced merging strategies.

  • Can I reset my progress?

    Yes, you can start over, but your upgrades and knowledge will help you advance faster the second time.

  • How often does the game get updated?

    The developers release frequent updates with new units, balance changes, and exciting events.
